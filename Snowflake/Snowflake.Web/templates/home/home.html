What is Snowflake? (Besides ice crystals from the sky.)

Snowflake is a site intended for people to toss thoughts out into the internet, and start conversations. Think of it as Tinder for thoughts.

So how does it work? See for yourself.

You post a thought off your profile. From there, you explore thoughts others have posted and sort them between ones you like and ones you dislike. When someone likes your thought, it provides the chance for you two to start talking in private. (See, it's Tinder.)

So why no pictures? Or other specific information?

The whole idea of Snowflake is to remove visual biases such as appearance and perceived gender from the starting equation and break the ice by seeing what is in someone's head.
